About Our Paintings

Our paintings are a non-traditional version of painting called pour paintings. Pour paintings are made by, you guessed it, pouring paint onto a canvas. In a more complicated sentence, you put different colors of paint in a cup. Before you pour the paint onto the canvas, you have to pour white paint on the canvas so the paint in the cup can slide around on the canvas easier. Tip-if you want to have lots of tiny bubbles on the canvas, use a blowtorch to blowtorch the paint.
